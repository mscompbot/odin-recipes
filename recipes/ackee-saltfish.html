Ackee & Saltfish

Image Source: Wikipedia Commons

Description

On this page you will learn to make Jamaica's national dish. Comprised of spicy flavous and soft textures, this is sure to be something you'll love.

Ingredients

  • 2 Canned Ackee
  • 1 lb Saltfish
  • 1 Onions Sliced
  • 1 Small tomato Diced
  • 1 Sweet bell pepper Or ½ red and ½ green like I did
  • 1 Stalk scallions Chopped
  • 2 Sprigs of thyme
  • ¼ Scotch bonnet pepper Seeds removed
  • ½ tsp Black pepper
  • 2 tbsp Cooking oil

Method

  1. Soak the saltfish for a minimum of two hours in cold water. Pour away the water.
  2. In a saucepan, place the saltfish and cover with fresh water. Put to boil on medium heat for 15 minutes.
  3. Removed from heat and pour away the hot water. Wash the fish in cold water to cool it.
  4. Remove the fish skin and debone the saltfish. Flake the saltfish and set aside.
  5. On medium fire, heat the oil in a cooking skillet. Add the onions, thyme, tomato, sweet pepper, scotch bonnet pepper, scallions and sauté for 3 minutes.
  6. Add the flaked saltfish and cook for another 3 minutes.
  7. Add the ackee, lower the heat and let it simmer for another 10-15 minutes
  8. Add the black pepper, turn the heat off and serve
